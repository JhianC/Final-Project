Seeing The World Through Words

To The Clouds Above

Seeking up above for something that you cannot find on the ground before you.

You see the natural glow that remains, the beauty that clouds your mind.

Despite being blinded by the rays of light you can still see right through.

The haze of your gaze, you'll eventually see what you're trying to find...

Unearthed

Each step you take will take you to where you should be.

The ground you walk upon decides the fate of an entire lifetime.

As though it seems that it's taking you to what you don't want to see.

As though it's setting up an infinite paradigm.

Walk not only with your feet but use your vision and your minds.
